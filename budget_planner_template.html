Welcome to Budget Planner

Track where your money goes each month and stay on the path to financial goals. Here's how to get started:

  1. Set your currency and monthly income in the bar above
  2. Add your fixed expenses (rent, subscriptions, loan payments)
  3. Add needs (groceries, transport) and wants (dining out, entertainment)

All data is saved automatically in your browser. Use Export CSV to back it up anytime.
